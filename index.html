The maker behind the magic

Meet Carol

Carol is a passionate crafter from Caerphilly, South Wales, with a true gift for turning everyday items into cherished keepsakes. Her journey started with a love of making things by hand — and that same heart goes into everything she creates today.

From vivid sublimation prints to cosy crochet and knitting, every piece is made with care, creativity and a personal touch. Whether it's a birthday cushion, a baby gift, or a personalised mug — Carol will make it special.

You'll often find Carol at Artisan markets in Merthyr Town Centre and Trago Mills, always with a smile and a stall full of colour.
